Ahoy!

Talk Like a Pirate Day be over, so it be time fer a review o' all tha' I prepared fer the scurvy bilge-rats who couldn't see it in person.

We be havin' a loop o' music jus' like the prior year, 'cept I not be able t' procure speakers this time so the music be softer (it be "He's a Pirate" from the Pirates o' the Caribbean soundtrack (http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7wxzuKwCXyk)- it be not a good idea t' mess wit' the classics). Thar' be a new poster hangin' out me window, flappin' in the breeze like a vessel's mainsail. I think tha' spot be an excellent place fer more decorations in the fut-Arrr. There be interior decorations here as well, wit' plenty o mini-posters copied from me ole work, an' it all be set up by 6:30AM, 'fore ev'n the most shipshape sailors be up. Finally, I be makin' tha' eyepatch look good.

The response to this year's TLaPD be excellent, wit' plenty o' pirate-y jibber-jabber from the crew, ole an' new, o' the good ship Nerd House. Thank ye all fer makin' this another memorable International Talk Like a Pirate Day. We be needin' t' disembark this here boat, but I hope ye'll all remember the joy tha' a good bit o' buccaneer blabbin' can bring. Be sure t' spread the word, an' if ye be confused, that word be: Yarrr!
